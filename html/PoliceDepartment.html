Police Department

The police department is the main law enforcement force in the city. The police officers are responsible for maintaining public order, preventing crime and protecting the citizens. The police department is divided into several units, such as patrol units, SPEED units, pilots, FBI and SWAT operators. The police officers are equipped with the latest technology and weapons to ensure the safety of the city. If you are interested in joining the police department, you can apply on our website or contact the police leader on Discord.
