Mechanics

The mechanics are the people who keep the city running. They are responsible for fixing cars, trucks, and other vehicles. They also provide maintenance services for the vehicles. Mechanics are essential for the city's infrastructure and economy. If you are interested in becoming a mechanic, join our discord server and apply for the job. We are always looking for new mechanics to join our team.
